Bio


I’m a Fullstack Software Engineer based in London, with a background in sales, design and filmmaking.


I have always been a curious tinkerer, teaching myself new skills in my spare time, because of my passion for creating and building new things.


After coding in my spare time for years, and following the developer community online, I slowly increased my knowledge of the tech ecosystem and I got hooked, this led me to decide to change career and soon after I joined the Software Engineering Immersive Bootcamp at General Assembly.


At my core, I am someone who loves working on interesting and complex challenges whilst also working as part of a team to collaboratively find solutions.
